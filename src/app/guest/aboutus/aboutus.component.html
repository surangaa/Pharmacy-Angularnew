About Us



How We Started

The owner, Mr. Ananda Weerasiri, has started this business in 2007. Their main business process is selling medical products such as medicinal drugs and medical equipment to customers. Buying pharmaceutical products from local agents of drug companies and maintaining stocks are also a part of their business process. Currently, two trainee sales girls are helping the owner in the process of issuing prescribed medicines to the customers.

We Are Trusted Company

"Weerasiri pharmacy is a pharmaceutical product seller who is registered under the National Medical Regulatory Authority (NMRA).
